Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

TrueSignal is designed with privacy as a core principle. This policy explains what information the app does and does not collect.

No Personal Information Collected

TrueSignal does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. No account is required to use the app. We do not collect your name, email address, location, device identifiers, or any other personally identifiable information.

How the App Works

TrueSignal works by sending a small network ping to a configurable server (by default, a reliable public server) and measuring the round-trip response time. This is the same mechanism your device uses to test connectivity. No data from these pings is stored on our servers — the result is displayed only on your device.

No Analytics or Tracking

TrueSignal does not include any analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or third-party tracking tools. We do not track how you use the app, how long you use it, or what features you access.

No Data Storage

All data displayed in TrueSignal — including connection status, latency readings, and recent history — is stored locally on your device only. Nothing is transmitted to TrueSignal servers. When you delete the app, all locally stored data is removed.

Configurable Ping Target

You can change the server that TrueSignal pings in the app settings. You may choose to use a local, regional, or private server for complete control over where ping requests are sent. We recommend using a server you trust and that is geographically close to you for the most accurate results.

Children's Privacy

TrueSignal does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app contains no features designed to collect data from any user, regardless of age.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. We will not change this policy in a way that reduces your privacy without clearly communicating the change.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at dave@developerd.com.
