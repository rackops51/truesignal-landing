Why TrueSignal

Built for people who need to know.

Ping-based accuracy

Active ping monitoring measures real-world latency to the server you choose. Not signal bars, not estimates, just the truth.

Plain-English diagnosis

Instead of raw metrics, TrueSignal explains what's happening in words. "Connection is slow. Latency is very high." No decoding required.

Network baselines

TrueSignal learns what's normal on each network you connect to, then flags when things are slower than usual. Not just slow. Slower than your normal.

Unified Timeline

How it works

Simple. Honest. Instant.

TrueSignal pings a reliable server every few seconds and measures the round-trip time. Under 150ms is green. Under 500ms is yellow. Anything else is red. No algorithms, no guessing, just the truth.

The Diagnosis card translates raw metrics into plain English. The Baselines engine learns what's normal on each of your networks. The Timeline captures every event, so you can prove what you experienced.
